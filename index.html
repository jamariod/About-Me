My name is Jamario Davis and I am a up and coming Atlanta based software developer who enjoys using code to bring creative thoughts to reality. I first developed a passion for code after my son was diagnosed with Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). After months of using Udemy, YouTube and Google I was able to create two iOS apps called “Autism Knowledge” which helps educate people about Autism and “What’s Your Choice” which helps those with disabilities to communicate. On August 31, 2019 I retired from the United States Navy after 20 years of active duty service. Since retirement I have focused my efforts on becoming a software developer and I choose to attend Digital Crafts not only to learn a valuable skill but to also be able to network with other likeminded individuals in the tech industry. Thank you for visiting my website and feel free to get in contact with me on Twitter.
